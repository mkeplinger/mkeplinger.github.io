--- layout: default title: Getting ready to deploy PushCommerce description: Getting ready to deploy PushCommerce published: true --- # Preparing your site to deploy PushCommerce? You will first need to go into your dashboard on http://yourstore.pushcommerceapp.com and grab your javascript embed code. Just replace `yourstore` with the shopify store name that usually is in the form of `yourstore.myshopify.com`.
